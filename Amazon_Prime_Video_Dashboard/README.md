# Amazon Prime Video Dashboard
Analyzed user engagement and content performance to identify top performing genres, viewing patterns, and regional trends. the goal was to provide actionable insights for
content strategy and marketing prioritization.

### Tools
-Microsoft Excel (data cleaning, dashboards, charts, KPI tracking).
-Pivot Tables and Excel formulas to summarize and analyze data.
-Charts and visualizationto highlight key trends.

### Insights
-Built an interactive Excel dashboards showing top genres and peak viewing times by region.
-Tracked viewer engagements and identified underperforming content.
-Enabled management to make data-driven decisions on content acquisition and promotion strategy.
